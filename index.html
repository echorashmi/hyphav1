How did we get here?

our story

We began our journey like most newbies: passionate, optimistic and excited.

But we soon ran into an oversaturation of unclear, sometimes contradictory information online, as well as an abundance of negative customer experiences.

To say the least, our early learning process felt like we were stumbling around in the dark, anxiously searching for a light switch.

Fast forward a few years — after plenty of trial and error we have decided to become that light switch for you.

And with that, hypha was born.
